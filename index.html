Sound demos for " Speaking inWavelet Domain: A Simple and Efficient Approach to Speed up Speech Diffusion Model"

Section I: Text-to-Speech task on the LJ Speech dataset

The audio samples are generated by conditioning on the ground-truth mel spectrogram, with a sampling frequency of 22050Hz. Let C denote the residual channels in the WaveNet-like architectures and T denote the number of diffusion steps in the reverse process. Because after different wavelets are accelerated, the quality of the sampling results is not much different from the quality of samples trained with the original speed diffusion, making it difficult to discern difference by listening. The third audio clip was deliberately chosen to showcase generated audio that doesn't perform very well (Low Speech Naturalness).
